Why multiple specialized models?

Instead of relying on a single general-purpose AI, we've developed and fine-tuned separate models for each critical aspect of hiring. This approach allows us to achieve higher accuracy in resume parsing, more nuanced job matching, better cultural fit assessment, and deeper market insights—resulting in matches that truly work for both candidates and companies.
