Behind the magic

Behind the playful design is a cutting-edge recommendation engine that treats hiring with the same intelligence as the best consumer apps.

Semantic breakdown

Vacancies and CVs are decomposed into skills, responsibilities, education, promotions, geographies, and more.

Instruction-tuned embeddings

Each dimension is encoded with AI prompts that capture intent, not just keywords.

Noise reduction

We standardize and enrich data to catch synonyms, misspellings, and hidden skills.

Multi-channel ranking

Candidates and roles are compared across six+ perspectives, then fused into a single, fair relevance score.

Human-in-the-loop controls

Recruiters adjust section weights in real time → results re-rank instantly.

Explainability baked in

Every match shows why it fits, not just a score.

The result? Matches that feel effortless on the surface, but are backed by AI rigor that outperforms naive LLM/embedding matchers.
